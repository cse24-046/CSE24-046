About Evodia

Evodia Perfumes is more than a brand — it's a lifestyle rooted in sensory exploration and sophistication. Established in 2025, Evodia was founded by a group of fragrance connoisseurs who believed everyone should experience the full fragrance pyramid — from fleeting top notes to the rich, grounding base.

We craft and curate luxurious perfume experiences that elevate confidence, allure, and individuality. Our scents are formulated with rare ingredients sourced from across the globe — and are designed to evolve beautifully throughout your day.

Our Mission

At Evodia, our mission is simple: to make premium, layered fragrance experiences accessible, educational, and enjoyable. We aim to:

  • Educate: Help users understand fragrance composition and layering.
  • Empower: Enable customers to choose scents that match their personality and lifestyle.
  • Enhance: Enrich the fragrance discovery process with trials and subscriptions.

Why Choose Evodia?

With Evodia, you don’t just wear a scent — you discover a story in every spray. Here’s what makes us different:

  • Premium Ingredients: Each fragrance uses ethically sourced, rare ingredients.
  • Full Note Journey: Enjoy every level of the fragrance — including the elusive base notes.
  • 14-Day Free Trial: No payment needed to begin exploring.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Tailored scent suggestions based on your preferences.
